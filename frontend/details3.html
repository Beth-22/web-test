Book Details

The Willoughbys

By Lois Lowry

The Willoughbys by Lois Lowry is a quirky and darkly humorous tale about a family of four siblings—Tim, Jane, Barnaby A, and Barnaby B—who are neglected by their eccentric and neglectful parents. The Willoughby children, tired of their parents' disinterest, decide to abandon them and create their own version of a "perfect" family. As they navigate their adventures, they encounter a series of unusual characters, including a nanny, a baby, and even a wealthy couple, all while discovering what family really means. Written in a whimsical and satirical style, the book cleverly pokes fun at the traditional family structure while exploring themes of love, belonging, and the importance of kindness. Through its witty narrative and entertaining characters, The Willoughbys captures the resilience of children and the transformative power of family.
